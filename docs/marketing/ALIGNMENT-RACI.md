# Alignment RACI Matrix

> RACI = Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed.
>
> Use this matrix to eliminate ownership ambiguity during alignment and execution.

---

## Role Key

- **Founder/CEO**
- **Marketing Lead**
- **Growth Lead**
- **Product Lead**
- **Ops / PMO**
- **Finance Owner**
- **Legal / Compliance**

---

## Core Alignment Activities

| Activity | Founder/CEO | Marketing Lead | Growth Lead | Product Lead | Ops / PMO | Finance Owner | Legal / Compliance |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Decide primary market focus | **A** | C | C | C | R | I | I |
| Approve quarterly message architecture | **A** | **R** | C | C | I | I | C |
| Finalize canonical pricing table | A | C | C | I | I | **R** | C |
| Finalize Pro economics (margin/commission) | A | C | **R** | C | I | **R** | C |
| Approve compliance baseline for guest capture | A | C | I | C | I | I | **R** |
| Select dominant growth loop for quarter | **A** | C | **R** | C | I | I | I |
| Approve launch playbook and owner map | A | **R** | **R** | C | **R** | I | I |
| Define KPI cadence and owners | A | C | **R** | C | **R** | I | I |
| Maintain decision log and audit trail | I | I | I | I | **R/A** | I | I |
| Run weekly alignment scorecard | I | C | **R** | I | **R** | I | I |
| Manage contradiction reconciliation backlog | A | C | C | C | **R** | **R** | C |
| Approve path worksheet sign-off | **A** | R | R | R | R | C | C |

---

## Escalation RACI

| Escalation Trigger | Responsible | Accountable | Consulted | Informed |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Priority 0 decision blocked > 7 days | Ops / PMO | Founder/CEO | Relevant function lead | Leadership team |
| Pricing changed without downstream sync | Finance Owner + Ops / PMO | Founder/CEO | Marketing Lead, Growth Lead | Leadership team |
| KPI owner missing for core metric | Growth Lead | Founder/CEO | Ops / PMO | Leadership team |
| Conflicting public narrative detected | Marketing Lead | Founder/CEO | Growth Lead, Product Lead | Leadership team |
| Compliance issue in capture/messaging flow | Legal / Compliance | Founder/CEO | Product Lead, Marketing Lead | Leadership team |

---

## Practical Rule

If an activity has no clear **A** and **R**, do not start execution.
