- **1 = strongly disagree**
- **3 = partially true**
- **5 = strongly agree**

## Interpretation

| Score Range | Readiness Level | Meaning | Required Action |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 42–50 | High | Strong alignment and execution readiness | Continue path and optimize |
| 34–41 | Moderate | Generally aligned but with notable risk pockets | Fix weakest 2 dimensions in next 7 days |
| 25–33 | Low | Alignment gaps likely to impair execution quality | Run alignment workshop before scaling |
| <25 | Critical | Team not execution-ready on current strategy | Pause expansion and reset Priority 0 decisions |
