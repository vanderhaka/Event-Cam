# Alignment Glossary

> Canonical terminology to avoid cross-team confusion in strategy and reporting.

---

## Core Strategy Terms

## Primary Market
The single main customer segment prioritized for the current planning horizon (typically 90 days to 6 months).

## Dominant Growth Loop
The one growth mechanism intentionally prioritized for scaling in the current quarter.

## Support Loop
A secondary loop that supports the dominant loop but does not compete for primary focus.

## Boundary (“Not Doing”)
A consciously deprioritized initiative for the current quarter to protect execution focus.

---

## Funnel Terms

## Visitor
A unique user/session reaching owned properties (site/app) within the reporting window.

## Signup
A newly created account in the product within the reporting window.

## Event Created
An event record successfully created by a user (regardless of payment status).

## Paid Event
An event with completed payment according to canonical billing status.

## Uploader
A unique participant who uploads at least one photo/video asset to an event.

## Upload Participation Rate
Unique uploaders divided by invited guests (or expected participants for open events).

---

## Channel & Partner Terms

## Pro Account
A professional account intended for partner-led event creation/referral motions.

## Pro Activation
A Pro account reaching first meaningful value event (paid event or converted referral).

## Referral Conversion
A referred prospect that becomes a paid event within attribution rules.

## Attribution Window
The defined time period in which a referral/marketing source receives credit.

---

## Economics Terms

## Canonical Pricing
The officially approved live pricing table used across all strategy and GTM docs.

## Contribution Margin
Revenue minus variable costs attributable to a specific event/customer.

## CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
Channel spend divided by channel-attributed paid customers/events.

## Payback Period
Time required for gross margin contribution to recover CAC.

---

## Alignment Operations Terms

## Priority 0 Decision
A path-defining decision that must be closed before expansion initiatives proceed.

## Decision Closure
A decision marked closed only when owner/date are logged and affected docs are synced.

## Superseded Decision
A previously closed decision replaced by a newer one, archived with rationale and evidence.

## Alignment Score
Weekly 0–20 score from `ALIGNMENT-SCORECARD.md` indicating alignment health.

## Documentation Sync SLA
Expected time (48 hours) to update affected docs after a closed decision.

---

## Usage Rule

If teams use different meanings for any term in this glossary, pause reporting and reconcile definitions before making decisions.
